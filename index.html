About Me

In the face of challenges, my approach involves meticulous problem-solving, breaking down complex issues into manageable components to solve them.

As the esteemed Stephen Hawking once said, "Intelligence is the ability to adapt to changes." This sentiment resonates deeply with me. The experience of acclimatizing to an entirely new environment has honed my adaptability. Undeterred, my aspirations propel me towards my next venture: residing in the United States.
